The Harry Potter Series

Orphan Harry learns he is a wizard on his 11th birthday when Hagrid escorts him to magic-teaching Hogwarts School. As a baby, his mother's love protected him and vanquished the villain Voldemort, leaving the child famous as "The Boy who Lived". With his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, Harry has to defeat the returned "One Who Must Not Be Named".This classic by JK Rowling would be remebered for its magical tale and characters. About the author

About the author...

J. K. Rowling, a British novelist and screenwriter who wrote the Harry Potter fantasy series. The books have won multiple awards, and sold more than 400 million copies. They have become the best-selling book series in history and been the basis for a series of films, over which Rowling had overall approval on the scripts and was a producer on the final films in the series.
